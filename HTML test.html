About me

My strengths are in Java and C++ primarily, and I have some experience with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have completed coursework in Computer Architecture, Formal Languages and Computations, Linear Algebra, Discrete Math, Data Structures and Algorithms, and Object Oriented Programming and Design. I have extensive experience using Github, which I have used to document nearly all of my projects from school projects, to personal projects, even to Codecademy projects. I have four years of work experience in the customer service industry while working as a lifeguard and supervisor at California's Great America. I worked as a lifeguard for two and a half seasons, then midway through my third season, I got promoted to supervisor. Because of my time at California's Great America as a supervisor, I have extensive leadership experience and know what it takes to lead a team of individuals.
